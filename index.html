CardOps

Scan, price & sell at shows

Coming soon to the App Store

CardOps is the inventory app for trading-card vendors. Scan cards with your camera, track what everything cost and what it's worth, and run your table at shows without depending on convention-hall wifi.

  • Card scannerPoint the camera at a card — on-device recognition matches it against a 20,000+ card catalog.
  • Show ModeOffline-first selling screen: instant search, big prices, one-tap sales, trades, and buys.
  • Shows & profitTrack every show's sales, expenses, and purchases — see per-show profit, cash flow, and ROI.
  • Market pricesCurrent market prices and history for every card in your inventory.
  • Show PrepBuild a bring list, download prices before you leave, and check off the packing list.
  • CSV import & exportYour data is yours — bring inventory in, take sales and expenses out.
